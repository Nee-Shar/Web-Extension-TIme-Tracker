Tips

1. Use a timer

Set a timer for each task you need to complete. This will help you stay focused and motivated to finish your work.

2. Engage in Offline Activities

Dedicate time to hobbies, exercise, reading, or spending time with friends and family offline. Engaging in these activities can help reduce reliance on screens for entertainment

3. Eliminate distractions

Identify the distractions that prevent you from focusing on your work. Try to eliminate or minimize these distractions to improve your productivity.

4. Limit Notifications

Disable non-essential notifications on your devices to minimize distractions and interruptions. Choose specific times to check messages and emails instead of reacting to every notification.

5. Practice Digital Detox Days

Schedule regular days or weekends where you disconnect from the internet entirely. Use this time to recharge, reconnect with nature, and engage in offline activities.
